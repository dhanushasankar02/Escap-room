First Timer FAQ

Everything you need to know before you arrive.

Are the rooms actually locked?

No! For safety reasons, the entrance door is always unlocked. You can leave at any time if you feel uncomfortable.

Is axe throwing safe?

100%. Our lanes are fully enclosed, and an Axe-pert stays with you the entire time to teach proper technique.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes! For axe throwing, closed-toe shoes are strictly required spot enforce safety rules.
