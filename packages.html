Event FAQ

Common questions about large bookings.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, all packages require a 50% non-refundable deposit to hold the date and secure the catering order.

When do you need a final headcount?

We require the final headcount 72 hours before your event. You will be billed for this number even if fewer people show.
