Roster FAQ

Everything you need to know about managing your team.

Can we add a player last minute?

Yes, as long as the total number of players does not exceed the room's maximum capacity. They must sign a waiver before playing.

How do we split the cost?

Our dashboard allows the captain to send payment requests to team members via email, or you can split it at the front desk.
