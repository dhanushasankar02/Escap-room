The Fine Print, Simplified

Our venue hosts two distinct high-adrenaline activities. Here's a quick summary of what you are agreeing to.

Escape Rooms

You agree to follow the Game Master's instructions, not to use excessive force on props, and acknowledge that loud noises and flashing lights may occur. You will not climb on furniture or break drywall.

Axe Throwing

You acknowledge the inherent risks of throwing sharp objects. You agree to wear closed-toe shoes at all times, only retrieve axes when instructed, and follow the strict zero-tolerance alcohol.

Under 18? Guardian Consent Required

All participants under the age of 18 MUST have their waiver signed by a legal parent or guardian. No exceptions. If a minor arrives without a completed guardian waiver, they will not be allowed to participate.

Axe Range Safety & Gear

Safety is our top priority. Before you step into the lane, you'll need to pass our mandatory safety briefing and ensure you have the proper gear:

  • Mandatory Footwear: Closed-toe shoes are required at all times.
  • Protective Gear: Safety glasses are available and recommended for all.
  • Safe Handling: Never hand an axe to someone; always place it in the block.
  • Throwing Box: Only one person is allowed in the throwing box at a time.
  • Expert Guidance: Listen to your Axe-pert. Their word is law.
